3. Five evidence views

Client value

What changed in the client's work? How is the claimed value measured, validated, and sustained?

Run health

What succeeded, failed, degraded, recovered, or required human intervention?

Adoption

Is the workflow actually being used and completed? Does the process owner trust it?

Economics

What are the delivery cycle, support burden, capacity demand, cost drivers, and reuse contribution?

Learning

What rule changed, failure was removed, pattern was packaged, or stop decision was made?

Evidence quality

Are definitions, baselines, units, owners, and source confidence clear enough to decide?
